# Product Overview

## Target Audience
Thai learners who want to study English in a friendly, modern, and trustworthy environment. The platform aims to be easy to use and encouraging, helping users stay motivated to continue learning.

## Product Personality
- **เป็นมิตร (Friendly)**: Welcoming tone, approachable design.
- **ทันสมัย (Modern)**: Clean, up-to-date UI patterns.
- **น่าเชื่อถือ (Trustworthy)**: Professional structure, clear progression.
- **ใช้งานง่าย (Easy to use)**: Intuitive navigation, low cognitive load.
- **ให้กำลังใจ (Encouraging)**: Celebrates progress, motivates users to keep learning.

## Core Directives
- Support both Thai and English content seamlessly.
- Ensure high readability across both Desktop and Mobile devices.
