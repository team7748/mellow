# Design System

## Design Direction
- **Primary Color**: Green.
- **Backgrounds**: White or Light Green to maintain a clean, airy feel.
- **Illustrations**: 2D illustrations with depth (ภาพประกอบ 2D มีมิติ).
- **Typography & Layout**: High legibility for both Thai and English. Fully responsive and readable across Desktop and Mobile.
- **Aesthetic**: Natural, crafted, and trustworthy. It must **not** look like a generic AI-generated dashboard.

## Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)
- ❌ **Cards within cards (การ์ดซ้อนการ์ด)**: Keep hierarchy flat and clean to avoid visual clutter.
- ❌ **Heavy shadows (เงาหนัก)**: Avoid stark drop shadows. Use flat design or extremely soft, subtle elevation.
- ❌ **Unnecessary gradients (Gradient ที่ไม่จำเป็น)**: Keep backgrounds solid and clean unless a gradient serves a specific functional or subtle aesthetic purpose.
- ❌ **Glassmorphism**: Avoid frosted glass effects; they distract from the content.
- ❌ **Excessive border radius (มุมโค้งมากเกินไป)**: Use sensible rounding. Avoid pill shapes for large structural containers.
- ❌ **Equal visual weight (ทุกองค์ประกอบมีสีเด่นเท่ากัน)**: Establish clear primary, secondary, and tertiary visual hierarchy. Not everything should grab attention equally.
- ❌ **Everything in a box (ทุก Section อยู่ในกล่อง)**: Allow some content to flow naturally on the page background without being artificially constrained inside a card.
